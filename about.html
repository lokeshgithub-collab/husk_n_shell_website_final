Our Vision & Mission

At HUSK N SHELL, we believe in transforming waste into wonders. Our mission is to promote sustainable living by offering handcrafted products made from coconut shells and husks. We aim to eliminate plastic and bring eco-conscious alternatives to households around the world.

How are the products made?
All products are handcrafted by local artisans using reclaimed coconut shells and husks.
Are the products washable?
Yes! They can be gently washed with warm water and mild soap. Avoid dishwashers for longer life.
